# Residual Analysis

Predicted sales values were calculated using the final multiple regression model.

Residuals were calculated as:

Residual = Actual Sales − Predicted Sales

## Largest Positive Residuals

The stores with the largest positive residuals achieved higher sales than predicted by the model. This may indicate strong local demand, effective store management, or other factors not captured in the dataset.

## Largest Negative Residuals

The stores with the largest negative residuals achieved lower sales than predicted by the model. This may indicate operational issues, local competition, or temporary market conditions.

## Business Interpretation

The model generally performs well but cannot capture every factor affecting store performance. Some stores are systematically under-predicted or over-predicted, suggesting that additional variables may improve model accuracy in future analyses.
