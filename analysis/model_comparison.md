# Model Comparison

## Model 1: Marketing Spend

Dependent Variable: Monthly Sales

Independent Variable: Marketing Spend

R-squared: 0.167

Marketing spend has a positive and statistically significant relationship with monthly sales. However, the model explains only 16.7% of sales variation, indicating that additional factors influence performance.

## Model 2: Footfall

Dependent Variable: Monthly Sales

Independent Variable: Footfall

R-squared: 0.736

Footfall is a strong predictor of monthly sales. The model explains 73.6% of sales variation and has strong business relevance.

## Model 3: Multiple Regression

Dependent Variable: Monthly Sales

Independent Variables:

* Marketing Spend
* Footfall
* Inventory Availability Percentage
* Region North Dummy Variable

R-squared: 0.805

This model provides the highest explanatory power. Marketing spend, footfall, and inventory availability are statistically significant. The Region North variable is not statistically significant.

## Final Model Selection

The multiple regression model was selected because it explains approximately 80.5% of the variation in monthly sales and includes multiple business drivers.
